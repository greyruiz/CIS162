I've Recently Gotten Back into K-Pop...

And I'm having a lot of fun with it! Back around 2015-2016, I used to watch a streamer on twitch draw her art comissions. She played some of the prettiest music i had ever heard at the time. I guess most were from popular korean drama's. I'm not going to pretend I know a lot about music, but the melodies/harmonies/chord progressions were so pleasing to my ears. I Ended up going down a small journey of discovering some K-Pop and R&B. My taste in music hasn't changed too much since then, but i definitely have been listenting to more pop and western artists in general.

However recently I dove back in to K-Pop per my friends reccomendation, and I'm having a blast. She's Been asking me to check out a girl group called 'Twice', and I finally did!

I... Am... A D D I C T E D

I am now in the shallow end of an insanely deep pool of music, and I'm so excited to check out more artists! On top of that, I'm rediscovering some old tracks I used to listen too back in the day (I get very sentimental about my music around that time) and it just makes me smile and reminisce.

Some Reccomendations

I know this is a strange comparison, but the satisfaction I get from a great video game OST and K-Pop is the same. While they differ in style, there's a certain structure and musicality that really stands out to me.
